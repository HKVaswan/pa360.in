About PA360.in

PA360.in is the official institutional arm of Project Athlete 360. We facilitate partnerships, manage research publications, and drive scientific innovation to elevate athlete performance and sports science across India. Join us in building a data-driven, collaborative future for every athlete and institution.

Why This Exists

What Drives This Movement?

Project Athlete 360 began with a single mission — to transform the sports system in India. Not just through awareness, but through execution. Our founder, H.K. Vaswan, once dreamed of being an athlete but lacked guidance and resources. That experience turned into a fire to build what he — and thousands of others — never had.

This platform exists for:

  • • Youths who lack access to scientific training
  • • Institutions with potential but no visibility
  • • Athletes who are talented but unsupported
  • • Sports researchers who want impact, not just publication

We are not here to fit in. We are here to build the missing system.

🔬 Science-Backed

We prioritize evidence-based training, sports biomechanics, psychology, nutrition, and injury prevention.

🤝 Youth & Institutional Focused

We bridge youth ambition and institutional capacity through collaboration, research, and real field impact.

🚀 A Movement, Not a Company

PA360 isn’t just a platform—it’s a mission to restore purpose, structure, and future to Indian sports.

🎯 Athlete-First Philosophy

Everything we create—content, systems, and solutions—puts the athlete’s journey at the center. We listen, we learn, and we build for real needs.
