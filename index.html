About PA360.in

PA360 Official — the institutional branch of Project Athlete 360, dedicated to research and partnerships.

PA360 Official is Project Athlete 360’s institutional branch focused on applied research, strategic partnerships, and evidence-based programs to elevate athlete performance. We’re launching as an introductory gateway to inform institutions and stakeholders about upcoming research, partnership opportunities, and applied projects.

Over the coming months we will assemble a dedicated team, run pilot studies, and open structured partnership channels for colleges, clubs, and federations.

As we grow, PA360 Official will manage research publications, facilitate collaborations, and drive scientific innovation across India — with a global perspective.

Join us in building a data-driven, collaborative future for every athlete and institution.

Note: Our research roadmap and the dedicated site are currently in the early planning phase; we’ll publish regular updates and partnership briefs soon.

Why This Exists

What Drives This Movement?

Project Athlete 360 began with a single mission — to transform the sports system in India. Not just through awareness, but through execution. Our founder, H.K. Vaswan, once dreamed of being an athlete but lacked guidance and resources. That experience became the fuel to build what many aspiring athletes and institutions never had.

This platform exists for:

  • Youths who lack access to scientific training
  • Institutions with potential but limited research capacity
  • Researchers seeking impact-driven applied studies
  • Coaches and practitioners who want usable, evidence-based solutions

We are here to build the missing system — practical, honest, and athlete-first.

🔬 Science-Backed

Evidence-based training, biomechanics, psychology, nutrition, and injury prevention.

🤝 Institution-Ready

Designed to partner with schools, colleges, and institutes for real-world impact.

🚀 Movement-Scale

Not just content — a mission to create lasting systems that scale across regions.

🎯 Athlete-First Philosophy

All work centers on practical athlete outcomes and sustainable development.
